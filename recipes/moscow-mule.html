Moscow Mule

Back to recipes

This classic Moscow mule recipe uses vodka, ginger beer, ice, and lime to create a refreshing cocktail!

Ingredients

  • 1½ fluid ounces vodka
  • ½ fluid ounce lime juice
  • Ice cubes
  • ½ cup ginger beer
  • Garnish: 1 lime wedge

Steps

  1. Pour vodka and lime juice into a mug; add ice cubes and ginger beer. Stir to combine.
  2. Drop a lime wedge into the mug for garnish.
  3. Serve!
