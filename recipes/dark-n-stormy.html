Dark 'N Stormy

Back to recipes

The Dark ’n Stormy is a delicious, easy-to-make cocktail that features a refreshing trio of dark rum, ginger beer and lime.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces Gosling’s Black Seal rum
  • 1/2 ounce lime juice, freshly squeezed
  • Ginger beer, to top (about 5 ounces)
  • Garnish: lime wheel

Steps

  1. Add rum and lime juice to a tall glass filled with ice
  2. Top with the ginger beer.
  3. Garnish with a lime wheel.
